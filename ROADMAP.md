# QualityTube OS Roadmap

## Phase 0 — Foundation Definition
- **Scope:** Define problem statement, user profile, and operating principles.
- **Outcome:** Shared product foundation and delivery constraints.

## Phase 1 — Repository & Environments
- **Scope:** Establish monorepo structure, local env, and service boot.
- **Outcome:** Reliable local development baseline.

## Phase 2 — Workflow Domain Modeling
- **Scope:** Define content lifecycle entities and state transitions.
- **Outcome:** Versioned workflow model for implementation.

## Phase 3 — Planning Module MVP
- **Scope:** Brief creation, topic queue, and editorial planning primitives.
- **Outcome:** Structured pre-production planning.

## Phase 4 — Research Assist Layer
- **Scope:** AI-assisted research support with source/citation fields.
- **Outcome:** Faster research with traceable inputs.

## Phase 5 — Script Drafting Module
- **Scope:** Draft generation pipeline with template controls.
- **Outcome:** Repeatable first-draft generation.

## Phase 6 — Quality Rubric Engine
- **Scope:** Introduce scoring/rubric checks for content quality.
- **Outcome:** Objective baseline for review decisions.

## Phase 7 — Compliance Rules Engine
- **Scope:** Implement YouTube policy-aware risk checks.
- **Outcome:** Early detection of compliance risk.

## Phase 8 — Human Review UX
- **Scope:** Reviewer workflows for approve/reject/request-change.
- **Outcome:** Clear human decision points.

## Phase 9 — Approval Audit Trail
- **Scope:** Immutable logging for decisions and rationale.
- **Outcome:** Accountability and postmortem support.

## Phase 10 — Asset & Metadata Packaging
- **Scope:** Titles, descriptions, tags, and packaging checklist.
- **Outcome:** Publish-ready metadata workflow.

## Phase 11 — Publishing Orchestration
- **Scope:** Scheduled and manual publish pipeline integration.
- **Outcome:** Controlled release execution.

## Phase 12 — Queue & Worker Hardening
- **Scope:** Idempotency, retries, timeouts, and failure policies.
- **Outcome:** Reliable asynchronous processing.

## Phase 13 — Role-Based Access Control
- **Scope:** Permissions for creators, reviewers, managers, admins.
- **Outcome:** Safer multi-user operation.

## Phase 14 — Notifications & SLA Signals
- **Scope:** Review queue notifications and stuck-item alerts.
- **Outcome:** Reduced workflow latency.

## Phase 15 — Analytics Ingestion
- **Scope:** Import performance metrics and normalize signals.
- **Outcome:** Queryable content performance dataset.

## Phase 16 — Feedback-to-Planning Loop
- **Scope:** Feed analytics insights into planning suggestions.
- **Outcome:** Continuous performance-informed iteration.

## Phase 17 — Cost & Quota Governance
- **Scope:** LLM spend controls and API quota guardrails.
- **Outcome:** Predictable operational economics.

## Phase 18 — Observability Baseline
- **Scope:** Logs, metrics, traces, and alert coverage.
- **Outcome:** Operational visibility across services.

## Phase 19 — Security Baseline
- **Scope:** Secret handling, scanning, auth hardening.
- **Outcome:** Reduced security risk surface.

## Phase 20 — Test Coverage Expansion
- **Scope:** Add integration and end-to-end workflow tests.
- **Outcome:** Higher release confidence.

## Phase 21 — Policy Regression Suite
- **Scope:** Create reusable compliance regression scenarios.
- **Outcome:** Stable policy-check behavior across releases.

## Phase 22 — Multi-Team Collaboration
- **Scope:** Workspaces, assignment, and ownership views.
- **Outcome:** Scalable team operations.

## Phase 23 — Release Management Controls
- **Scope:** Feature flags, staged rollout, rollback runbooks.
- **Outcome:** Safer production deployment lifecycle.

## Phase 24 — Pilot Readiness
- **Scope:** Harden docs, onboarding, and support workflows.
- **Outcome:** Controlled pilot deployment.

## Phase 25 — General Availability Readiness
- **Scope:** Final quality gates, SLO validation, and launch checklist.
- **Outcome:** Production-ready QualityTube OS release.
