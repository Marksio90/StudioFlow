# Product Positioning

## Positioning Statement

AI Media Operations OS is a workflow operating system for YouTube production teams.
It centralizes planning, AI-assisted content operations, human review, compliance, publishing, and analytics.

## Target users

- Solo creators scaling to a team
- Small in-house content teams
- YouTube-focused agencies

## Non-goals

- Not a one-click money generator
- Not autonomous channel farming automation
- Not a replacement for editorial decision-making
