# QualityTube OS Non-Goals

## Not a content spammer

QualityTube OS is not designed to maximize upload volume at the expense of viewer value. The system prioritizes editorial quality and policy-safe workflows over mass production.

## Not guaranteed monetization

The product does not guarantee revenue, RPM, subscriber growth, or monetization status. Outcomes depend on strategy, execution quality, audience fit, and external platform factors.

## Not passive income promise

QualityTube OS is not a passive-income system. It is an operations platform that still requires active planning, review, and decision-making by humans.

## Not low-effort AI video farm

The platform is intentionally incompatible with low-effort, repetitive AI content farming models. Quality controls and approval gates are designed to discourage that behavior.

## Not a replacement for human judgment

AI components assist with speed and structure, but human judgment remains mandatory for approval, risk evaluation, and final publishing accountability.
